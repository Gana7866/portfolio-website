About Me

Hello, I'm an artist passionate about creating visual art that inspires and evokes emotions. My work explores themes of nature, identity, and culture. Every landscape in this collection is one I've sat in front of, marveled at and savored. While I'm glad to have captured the comfort they've brought me, I hope you also get a taste of their vastness. The colors, lines, and shapes I use seek to emulate how otherworldly they seem in today's modern society.
